Planetary Alignment

Planetary alignment occurs when two or more planets appear in a straight line from Earth’s perspective. Though not perfectly aligned in space, these events create stunning visuals in the night sky.

Alignments can involve inner planets like Mercury and Venus or outer giants like Jupiter and Saturn. Rare alignments of multiple planets are significant astronomical events, often visible without telescopes.

Such alignments highlight the harmony of celestial mechanics and inspire observers to appreciate the vastness and precision of our solar system.
