The Moon

The Moon is Earth’s only natural satellite and has fascinated humans for centuries. It influences tides, marks celestial phases, and is a key focus of space exploration. From craters formed by meteor impacts to the mysteries of its far side, the Moon continues to inspire scientists and enthusiasts alike.

Modern missions aim to establish lunar bases, study resources like water ice, and prepare for deeper space travel. The Moon is not just a cosmic neighbor, but a stepping stone to humanity’s future among the stars.
